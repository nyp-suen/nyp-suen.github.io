What is Chess?

Chess is a game played by two players on a square board, in which each player has 16 pieces that can be moved on the board in different ways with the game ending in either one of the players checkmating the other or in a draw if checkmate is not possible.

History of chess

Chess was invented in India in the 6th century CE(Common Era) and its earliest known form is chaturanga. The pieces in it represents military units at the time. These include elephants,infantry and chariots.

The game spread throughout Asia and Europe over the coming centuries, and eventually evolved into what we know as chess around the 16th century.
