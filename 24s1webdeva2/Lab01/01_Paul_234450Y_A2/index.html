Pizza is a traditional Italian dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven. A classic favourite in Singapore is the Pepperoni Pizza. The pizza dough is layered with mozarella cheese, tomato paste and topped with delectable pepperoni slices.

Ingredients needed for classic Pepperoni Pizza

Pizza Dough Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour (160 g)
1 tsp yeast
1 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp salt
1 tbsp olive oil
3 fl oz warm water (90 ml)

Ingredients for Tomato Pizza Sauce:
14 oz tomatoes (400 g)
1 garlic clove
4-5 basil leaves
1 tbsp oregano
2 tbsp olive oil
Black pepper to taste
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp sugar
1 tbsp tomato paste

Pizza topping ingredients:
Pepperoni sausage slices
7 oz mozzarella cheese (200 g)
Slice some onion
Some sweet corn

Pizza Hut Information

Pizza Hut is a famous American pizza restaurant chain founded in 1958 by brothers Dan and Frank Carney in Wichita, Kansas. Pizza Hut was founded in Singapore in 1981. It quickly expanded and became part of Yum! Brands. Pizza Hut is known for its wide variety of pizzas, including popular options like Pepperoni and Supreme, with different crust types such as Original Pan, Hand-Tossed, and Stuffed Crust. Besides pizza, they offer pasta, wings, breadsticks, and desserts. The chain operates in various formats, including dine-in restaurants, carryout locations, and delivery services.
