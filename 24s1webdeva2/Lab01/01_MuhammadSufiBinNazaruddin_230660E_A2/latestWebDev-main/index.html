What are Numbers?
They are an arithmetical value, expressed by a word, symbol, or figure, representing a particular quantity and used in counting and making calculations. Generally used for quantity or amount.
What is Math exactly?
Make a calculation or come to a conclusion based on the relevant facts and figures, typically with the implication that the result is or should be obvious.
Who invented Mathematics
Archimedes is regarded as the founding figure of mathematics. But the question of who created mathematics has no clear answer. In many centuries and by many people, it was discovered. We think it's more accurate to say that humanity discovered mathematics and that mathematics belongs to the entire planet.
Why do we need Math?
Math helps us understand the world, and we use the world to understand math. The world is interconnected. Everyday math shows these connections and possibilities.
Why is Mathematics difficult.
Unlike other subjects that are more concrete, math deals with numbers, symbols, and equations that can be difficult to grasp.
Is it okay to struggle at Mathematics.
Even though most basic math could be learned by nearly anyone, finding difficulty in understanding math concepts is a common experience.
